01. About Me

Hello! I'm Graham, a producer and technical PM based in Carnation, WA. I've worked in the games industry since 2014 at AAA companies like Xbox, Blizzard Entertainment, andEpic Games.

I specialize in taking high-level direction and turning it into specific action for my teams. I believe teams work best when process is light but well understood, leaving specialists to do what they do best with minimal overhead.

Here are a few technologies and skills I work with:

  • Game Production
  • Java / C++ / C#
  • Vendor Management
  • Live Ops
